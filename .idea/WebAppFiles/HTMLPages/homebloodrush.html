Simple. Innovative. Compelling

Koala Tea Interactive made Bloodrush with a certain image in mind. This resulted in a perfect indie blend and making the game something for everybody to enjoy. It has simple, turn-based rules wrapped up in an endless survival and strategy package. This makes for quick gameplay and that "One more run" feeling, hunting for score to beat your friends!

This new mix of all those genres: turn based, stategy survival, lend it an innovative twist never seen before, landing itself between other indie classics and breaking the mold of all the cookie-cutter copy and paste jobs seen everywhere on the Play Store.

With many achievements to complete and randomly generated maps & quests, no two games will be the same. Monsters also spawn randomly and adopt all kinds of tactics, especially hard-to-beat ones once you start playing on the "hard" difficulty. With the easy to play, but hard to master nature, there's something to love for everyone! Still not satisfied? Well, the game also comes with all kinds of powerups, leaderboards and selectable skins so that you can go all in!
