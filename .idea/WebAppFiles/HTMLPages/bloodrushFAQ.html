Q: Is KTI a real company?


No. KTI is in no way a real company. It was a name the students coined for their project group and it has become their project alias ever since.

Do KTI's members still work together?


Yes! All four students have currently grouped up once more for their next project. Their new product will be a Who-Is-Who app for TRIMM.

Did KTI actually win any of the specified awards?


No. KTI or their apps have in no way participated in any competitions. The awards have been added to show extra content on several pages.

Are any of the contact points real?


No. None of the contact credentials featured on the "Contact us" page are actually owned by any of the team's members. Please do not try to contact these, as this might disturb any real users.

Is Bloodrush still being updated?


No. Bloodrush has received no further updates after the project's final deadline. The team has shown interest into perhaps rebuilding it, however.

Who made the art for Bloodrush?


The artist Victor Nedelchev made most of the art for Bloodrush. Any other art assets in Bloodrush were taken from Google and/or given to the team by the school.

Who made the promotional poster for Bloodrush?


Thobias Melchers is the artist who was responsible for the poster used to promote Bloodrush. His website can be found through Google by typing "www.thobiasm.nl"
